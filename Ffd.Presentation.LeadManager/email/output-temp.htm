Show support for your favorite athlete with our premium sports-themed jersey stickers!

Joseph, looking for something different than the same old cookie dough or scratch-off cards for your athletics' related fundraiser?  Raise money for your fundraiser as well as represent your favorite players with our new, completely custom jersey stickers!

Stickers are available in 5 major sports themes (baseball/softball, football, basketball, hockey, and soccer).  They're produced from 5-year exterior life vinyl and are about 5 1/2" x 5 1/2" (depending on design).  Also included are detailed application instructions.

Customization Options

You can customize any of the stickers (at no extra cost), or get them all the same.  They feature any name of your choice (up to 13 characters) and any number (up to 2 digits).  Typically you will use the name of the team with the year such as "Warriors 08", or individual players' name/nickname with their number.
